Advisory Board

The royalties received from the sale of the CDI Instruments are placed in a nonprofit account at the Sand Diego State University Foundation and are used to fund further research, assessment, and applications of the CDI instruments. Funding decisions are made by the board, which meets annually. The members of the board are:
